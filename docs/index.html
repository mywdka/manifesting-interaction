In Transformation Design 2.1, you learn the methods of interactive audio visual design; from composing, remixing, manipulating images, to scripting their interaction with your audience. This course provides the framework for you to develop your own (interactive) manifesto.

The course is composed of 12 classes: 5 on visual programming, 5 on sensing machines, and 2 feedback sessions. The classes include tutorials, weekly exercises, and contact moments reserved for support.
